The Inspiration:
Ryan and Robbie

Ryan and Robbie are part of a multicultural family: their mom is from China and their dad grew up in the United States. The brothers love to explore their neighborhood and go on adventures.

Ryan is six years old. He likes puzzles, swimming and the color blue. He's shy around new people, and is an excellent listener.

Robbie is four years old. He likes playing with action figures and his favorite color is green. He's a very curious boy; sometimes too curious!

The Author:
Roger Hackett

Roger Hackett was born and raised in culturally diverse San Antonio, Texas. He is multicultural himself: his ancestors were Irish, English, Swedish, German, African-American, English and Native American.

Roger joined the army before college, and, during his tour of duty, traveled to ten countries. He studied electrical engineering at Boston University, where he met his future wife, a native of Beijing, China. Following graduation, he worked as an engineer for Web TV, Toshiba, Microsoft, and Apple's iPod Division. During over twenty business trips to China, he honed his Mandarin Chinese.

Roger's two sons, and his passion for Chinese culture and language inspired him to write his first book: Ryan and Robbie's Bike Ride Adventure. He lives with his family in Washington State where he's hard at work on the next exciting Ryan and Robbie adventure.

The Illustrator:
Jared Beckstrand

Jared Beckstrand is the illustrator for the Ryan and Robbie book series. Jared has a background in animation and spent 10 years at Walt Disney studios as a character animator on 9 feature films. A few of his assignments were to bring ethnic characters to life such as Pocahontas, Esmerelda from Hunchback of Notre Dame, and Pacha from The Emperor's New Groove. He currently lives in southern Utah with his wife and four children. He can be found on the web at: www.jaredbeckstrand.com
